Coffee...

  1. Materials Required: Coffee beans, grinder, coffee maker, water, mug.
  2. Grind about 2 tablespoons of coffee beans.
  3. Add the ground coffee to the coffee maker.
  4. Fill the coffee maker with about 6 ounces of water.
  5. Turn on the coffee maker to start brewing.
  6. The coffee will start to drip. Wait for the brewing process to complete.
  7. Once brewed, pour the coffee into a mug.
  8. Add sugar or creamer according to your preference.
  9. Stir well and enjoy your freshly brewed coffee.
