Orange Juice Recipe yes

  1. Peel the oranges and remove any seeds.
  2. Cut the oranges into small pieces.
  3. Put the orange pieces into a blender.
  4. Add water and sugar to the blender.
  5. Blend the mixture until smooth.
  6. Strain the juice to remove any pulp.
  7. Chill the orange juice in the refrigerator.
  8. Serve the orange juice cold and enjoy!
