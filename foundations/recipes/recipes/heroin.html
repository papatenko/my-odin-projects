Herion Recipe

  1. Grow and harvest opium flower seed pods.
  2. Extract the opium from the pod.
  3. Create the morphine base.
  4. Make heroin from the morphine base, which would require:
    • Acetic anhydride
    • Chloroform
    • Sodium carbonate
    • Alcohol
    • Ether
    • Hydrochloric acid
  5. Cut and distribute.
